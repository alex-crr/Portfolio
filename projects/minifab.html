2.1 DESCRIPTION

Project Overview

Minifab is a highly customisable open source hybrid manufacturing machine capable of both 3D printing and CNC milling. Designed to bring professional-grade microfabrication capabilities to makers and small workshops.

The project focuses on creating a versatile platform that can switch between additive and subtractive manufacturing processes, enabling complex workflows that would typically require multiple machines.
