1.1 DESCRIPTION

Project Overview

My journey into the world of 3D printing began in 2018 when my parents bought me an Anet A6 kit. What started as a simple assembly project sparked a deep passion for additive manufacturing and ultimately helped shape my path toward engineering. This printer has been continually evolving as my knowledge and skills have grown.

The original Anet A6 has undergone significant modifications over the years. I upgraded the frame with an AM6 extrusion kit for improved stability and precision. I also transitioned from the stock firmware to Marlin firmware, and then to Klipper, allowing for more advanced features and better print quality.

While not the fastest or most advanced printer by today's standards, this machine has immense personal value. It represents my growth as a maker and engineer, showing how continuous iteration and learning can transform both a project and the person behind it.

1.3 OUTCOMES

This project has been instrumental in my development as an engineer:

Programming and firmware customization to optimize machine performance

This printer was one of the key inspirations that led me to pursue engineering as a career path.
