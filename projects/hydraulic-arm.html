Project Description

The hydraulic robotic arm project focused on creating a highly precise manipulation system for industrial applications. Unlike traditional electric actuators, our hydraulic system provides superior force density and smooth operation, making it ideal for heavy lifting tasks in constrained environments.

The 6-degree-of-freedom arm incorporates force feedback at each joint, allowing for delicate operations like egg handling while still maintaining the strength to lift 5kg payloads. We developed a custom control algorithm that combines PID control with adaptive damping to achieve millimeter-level positioning accuracy.
